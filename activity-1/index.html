I will be listing out steps for how to make chicken parmesan.

  1. Mix bread crumbs, parmesan cheese, and italian spices on a plate.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together 4 eggs
  3. Put some olive oil in a pan and put on Medium heat and preheat oven to 350 degrees
  4. Prepare chicken breast by cutting them in half and beating them
  5. Dip chicken in egg wash, then bread crumb mix
  6. Put chicken in pan for 2 min on each side and then put on a cookie sheet
  7. Lay some motzarella cheese and marinara sauce on the chicken
  8. Bake for 15 min

After completing all of the steps grab a piece of chicken parm and enjoy.
